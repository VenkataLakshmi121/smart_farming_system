Prevention

  • This fungal leaf spot disease is caused by Alternaria polyantha. The disease is characterized by the appearance of brown spots with faint concentric rings on the mid-rib.
  • Control - The disease can be controlled by the spray of Bordeaux mixture (0.4%), Zineb (0.5%) or Mancozeb (0.2%) or Iprodione (0.2%) at 10 days interval.The other diseases reported are rust and powdery mildew, which are not serious. Suitable fungicides may be administered to control them when the damage is noticed.
